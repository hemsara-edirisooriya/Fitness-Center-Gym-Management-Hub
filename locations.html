How to reach us

Our gym is conveniently located in the heart of the city, making it easy to access from anywhere. If you’re coming by bus, several main routes stop just a short walk away.
The nearest train station is only a few minutes from our entrance, and taxis or ride-sharing services can drop you right at the front gate.
For those driving, we offer spacious free parking for members, so you can arrive and start your workout without any stress.
You’ll also find nearby landmarks like the central mall and city park, which make it easy to spot us even if it’s your first visit.
